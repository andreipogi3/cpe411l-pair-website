Our History

Pinoy Car Rentals was founded in 2024 with a simple vision: to make transportation easy, affordable, and accessible for Filipinos across Luzon. We started as a small group of car enthusiasts who believed that everyone deserves a smooth and worry-free travel experience — whether it's for a family road trip, business trip, or spontaneous getaway.


From just 3 sedans in Bulacan, we've now grown into a trusted local car rental brand serving various cities and provinces. Our fleet now includes a wide range of vehicles — from economy cars to SUVs and vans — carefully maintained and ready for adventure.


We’re proudly Filipino-owned and operated, and we continue to deliver service with puso. Our commitment is to provide reliable cars, easy booking, and great customer service every time you hit the road with us.
